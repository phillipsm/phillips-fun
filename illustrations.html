Illustrations

Simple drawings can instantly explain a complex topic. They can provide energy and personality to interfaces.

Most of my graphics start off as pencil and paper sketches, then I'll place photos of the sketches in Illustrator and work on stroke weight, color and balance. The more raw and gutter punk, the better.
